My unofficial birding blog

I have been posting some recent sightings. This was created in 2023.

2023:

January:

On New Year's day we went to Metung, Victoria. First sighting of the year Australian Pelican swimming round in a farm dam passing by. Suspected Blue-faced honeyeater (very out of range) lurking around in tree at Willow Park, Rosedale, Gippsland, VIC. When we got to Metung Australian Magpie, Crested Pigeon. Going down to lake king in afternoon on the way Common bronzewing and Black swans, chestnut teal, little pied cormorant, silver gull, australian white (sacred) ibis and white-faced heron in the lake. Galah, Little corella, welcome swallow and rainbow lorikeet on the way back.

Little wattlebird later in the day

Next day crimson rosella, brush bronzewing, eurasian blackbird and magpie lark all present. Wood duck at Lake king. Outing at Nyerimilang heritage park, nungurner provides sightings of Wonga pigeon, White-faced heron, grey fantail, superb fairywren, masked lapwing, brown thornbill.

Various outings to jetty provide sightings of water rats, aust. pelicans and a very exciting Peregrine falcon present.

Going to raymond island provides sightings of crested terns, little black cormorants, crimson rosella and musk lorikeet.

forgotten national park provides azure kingfisher and yellow-tailed black cockatoo.

Bairnsdale wetlands Swamp harrier, redbrowed finch and whitebrowed scrubwren to mention a few.

Outing to lakes entrance provides eastern whipbird. Also long-billed corella and sulphur-crested cockatoos sighted in Paynesville.

Pied currawong, house sparrow and suspected king parrot sighted Cann river on way to Jindabyne.

Jindabyne

In jindabyne we come across many species. getting there black-faced cuckoo-shrike, shortly after yellow-rumped thornbill going back dusky woodswallow australian magpie, eastern rosella nankeen kestrel. Mount kosciuszko provides sightings of australian raven, australasian pipit and australian hobby.

Something like little friarbird later. Flame robin and striated pardalote sighted on the campsite.

Some crimson rosellas on way to dead horse gap.

Wedge-tailed eagle on way to "copper tom trail" on lake jindabyne

Rufous whistler sighted on campsite later as well as eastern rosella.

Suspected emu sighted on the way to Thredbo

finally grey currawong again in the campsite. This site provides lots of good birds!

got home and grey butcherbird appearance on clothesline.

Brolgas, white-fronted chats, australian shelducks, brown falcon, fairy martin, yellow-billed spoonbill,common starling and royal spoonbill to mention a few at WTP (western treatment plant, Werribee)


February:

Rock doves and whatnot around house

Little corella shown


March:

Yellingbo lots of honeyeaters: white-naped, white-eared, helmeted, yellow-faced etc. at yellingbo. Also GST (grey shrike thrush), white-faced heron, little-pied cormorant, rufous fantail, grey fantail, rufous whistler, golden whistler, white-throated treecreeper, eastern rosella, eastern yellow robin and various other birds.

Crested pigeon, aust. magpie, rainbow lorikeet, little corella in our garden

driving around rainbow lorikeet, pied currawong, spotted dove and indian myna. Little wattlebird in our garden.

Point cook outing little-pied cormorant, pacific gull, black-faced cormorant, collared sparrowhawk, white-plumed honeyeater, european goldfinch and many others.

April:

WTP again with sightings of straw-necked ibis and horsefields bushlark as new birds with little egret, great egret, black-winged stilt, red-necked stint, australian spotted crake, brolga, white-faced heron, silver gull, magpie lark, little-pied cormorant, little black cormorant, chestnut teal, hoary headed grebe, little raven, pink-eared duck, welcome swallow, black swan, crested tern, common tern and welcome swallow to name a few.

Port fairy with crested turn, ruddy turnstone, sooty oystercatcher, white-faced heron, unknown honeyeater, silvereye, various gerygones, brown thornbill, NHH (new holland honeyeater), red-browed finch, welcome swallow, kelp gull etc.

On the way to budj bim NP grey goshawk (light morph), then nankeen kestrel, hoary-headed & australasian grebes, striated thornbill, australasian darter, musk duck, hardhead (white-eyed duck), superb fairywren, sulphur-crested cockatoo.

Silver gull, black swan, both grebes, NHH, white-faced heron, common starling, masked lapwing, eurasian coot, willie wagtail, dusky moorhen, swallows etc.

Yambuk lake whistling kite sighted with little egret, pied oystercatcher, hoary=headed grebe, stints, ducks, various waterfowl like coots and moorhens.

Suspected fairy tern and singing honeyeater in various beaches at port fairy.

Eastern rosella, spotted dove, magpie lark, pied currawong, noisy miner and rainbow lorikeet at Surrey Park.

rock dove, australian magpie, rainbow lorikeet, little raven, noisy miner, grey butcherbird at our house.

Silver gull, rock dove, house sparrow, indian myna, red wattlebird, white-plumed honeyeater, eurasian coot, dusky moorhen, magpie lark, spotted dove all sighted in city around Fed square and corresponding yarra river. (white-plumed was sighted in federation square).
